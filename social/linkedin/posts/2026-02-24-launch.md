We built an open standard for fitness progression. Here's why.

Every gym has "Rx" athletes and "beginners," but what does that actually mean? There's no shared definition. A beginner at one gym might be intermediate at another.

OpenProgression fixes that.

We analyzed 1.3 million data points from peer-reviewed research to create 7 clearly defined fitness levels across 8 categories, from Squatting to Olympic Lifting to Endurance.

Every benchmark traces back to published studies. Every number is defensible. And the entire thing is free and open source.

What you get:
- 7 levels mapped to population percentiles (Beginner through Rx)
- 25 benchmarks across 8 fitness categories
- A level calculator to assess where you stand
- Scaled programming with workouts for every level
- All data available as JSON for developers

Whether you're a coach placing new members, an athlete tracking progress, or a developer building fitness software, the standard is yours to use.
